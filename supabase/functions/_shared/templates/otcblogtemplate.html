Blue Candy

Blue candy buffets have surged in popularity thanks to their visual appeal and versatility. Whether you're planning a winter wonderland event or a boy-themed birthday party, blue treats offer both elegance and fun.

Color coordination is key to a memorable candy buffet, and blue offers a stunning range — from pastel baby blues to deep navy tones. Mixing different shades of blue adds visual interest and helps tie in other decor elements, like balloons, banners, or tablecloths.[1] Accent the display with silver or white accessories for an icy, elegant touch, or add pops of yellow and orange for a more playful, high-energy vibe.

Don't forget to balance your display with both form and function. Use risers and stands to create height variation, and clearly label each type of candy to make the experience easy and enjoyable for guests. With Oriental Trading's wide assortment of blue sweets and display essentials, even first-time party planners can create a buffet that looks professionally styled — without the professional price tag.

Theme & Event Ideas

One of the best parts about creating a candy buffet is the interactive element it brings to your event. Guests love choosing their favorite sweets, snapping photos of the display, and filling up take-home treat bags. It adds both decoration and entertainment — a win-win for party hosts looking to make their event stand out without overcomplicating the planning process.

Whether you're organizing a baby shower, a graduation party, or a gender reveal, a blue candy buffet delivers visual wow-factor [3] and nostalgic fun. And thanks to Oriental Trading's curated selection of candies, containers, and coordinating accessories, putting one together is easier — and more affordable — than ever. Your only challenge? Choosing which treats to include!

Blue candy fits effortlessly into many party themes:

  • Winter Wonderland or Ice Princess
  • Gender Reveal Parties (Team Blue!)
  • Beach, Ocean, or Nautical Themes
  • Graduations and School Events

Party Planning Tip

Need to estimate how much candy you'll need? Plan for about 8 ounces of candy per guest — that's roughly a small favor bag filled with a mix of treats. For larger jars or bulk displays, order a little extra to keep the buffet full and photo-ready throughout your event.
